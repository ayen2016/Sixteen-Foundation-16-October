Al Zakirin School

In the Summer of 2023, we supported Al Zakirin School situated in a village called Mehreeba in Al Jazeera, Sudan.

Thanks to all our donors.
This campaign is now closed.

It is run by Shaykh Abd Al Majeed and there are more than 50 of these mud and thatch schools. Despite the ongoing war in this region, this remarkable man is ensuring that his students still have access to education. With your support, they are preparing the ground for rebuilds of damaged structures and have begun the build of a new classroom in the school complex. Your donations have gone towards all the material costs and labour of this build. The plan is to use this new build as a space for the children to recite and memorise the Qur’an. Moving from the mud and thatched buildings will provide safety and coverage from the elements during the difficult seasons. This enables the students to sit, sheltered from the elements and concentrate on their studies. The community are overwhelmed by our support and thank all involved from the bottom of their hearts.

Highlights

1. The school, run by Shaykh Abd Al Majeed, is one of over 50 mud-and-thatch schools in the region.

2. Despite ongoing conflict, he ensures students continue to access education.

3. Donations support rebuilding damaged structures and a new classroom for Qur’an recitation and memorisation.

4. The new build provides safety from the elements, allowing students to study comfortably, and the community is deeply grateful.

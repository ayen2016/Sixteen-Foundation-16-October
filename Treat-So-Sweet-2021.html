Treat so Sweet 2021

‘Treat-so-Sweet’: Delightful Cheesecakes Across the Northwest

Treat so Sweet 2021

Date: April 13, 2021 | Place: North West , Stockport & Blackburn | Organiser: Muntaha Begum

‘Treat-so-Sweet’ was led by Muntaha Begum and her team Saima and Sadiya in the Northwest region. Each cheesecake was priced at £20 with individual packaging and personalised tags. Ingredients were sourced in advance with the appropriate branded items advertised. Customised orders included 4” cheesecake priced at £10 and 10” priced at £30. Orders were made through Google Form with feedback to engage with customer satisfaction.

Our deliveries took us across the North West from Stockport to Blackburn. We thoroughly enjoyed meeting all the faces behind the orders and the positive feedback received from our cheesecakes. This was definitely the ‘cherry on top!’

Al Rawda Educational Institution

In the Summer of 2021 we supported Al Rawda Educational Institution.

Thanks to all our donors.
This campaign is now closed.

In the Summer of 2021 we supported Al Rawda, an educational institution in London which offers a range of educational courses and programs for people of all ages and walks of life. We worked alongside the Chief Executive at Al Rawda to continue his range of publications. He went on to compile, produce and distribute the publication of “Prophetic Wellbeing: 40 Hadith on Healthy Living.” These books have been distributed to the local community and stored in the library for students to access and read. The publication alongside others are available to purchase online, we were lucky enough to receive complimentary copies. The publication has received 5 star reviews by those who have had the opportunity to read and provides some beautiful nuggets for the reader to take away them. We were also fortunate enough for Al Rawda to a include a little dedication note for our beloved parents, which was a lovely gesture. “For our beloved mother and father of sixteen children: always wise in counsel, tender in judgement, believers in Islam and fervent in charity. We dedicate, with reverence, these special books.”

Highlights

1. In Summer 2021, Sixteen Foundation supported Al Rawda, an educational institution in London offering courses for all ages.

2. We collaborated with the Chief Executive to produce and distribute “Prophetic Wellbeing: 40 Hadith on Healthy Living.”

3. The books were shared with the local community, stored in the library, and are available online, receiving 5-star reviews.

4. A special dedication was included for the parents of Sixteen Foundation, honoring their guidance and charity.
